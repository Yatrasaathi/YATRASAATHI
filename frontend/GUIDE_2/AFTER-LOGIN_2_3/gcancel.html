Cancellation Policy for Guides

  • When cancelling a confirmed booking, you won't receive any payment and your chance to receive future bookings will be decreased so rethink before cancelling.
  • If tourist cancel booking within 24hr, then there will be no problem for guide and their availability status and rate of getting chances to receive future bookings will not be hampered.
  • In case of same day booking means the starting time begins within 24hr of booking then the guide will receive full amount.
  • In case of emergency occurs, the guide can switch his confirmed booking to another guide only if 6hr before the arrival of tourist and must notify us.
  • All bookings must be confirmed within 6hr. If you reject within the specified time range the booking request expires and no payment is made. This also counts when the tourist withdraws booking request before you confirm it.
