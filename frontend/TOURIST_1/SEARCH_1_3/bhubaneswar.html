Bhubaneswar

Bhubaneswar, the capital of Odisha, is known for its ancient temples, rich culture, and modern infrastructure. Explore the spiritual and historical essence of this beautiful city. It serves as a hub for education and information technology in eastern India. The city's blend of historic architecture and modern infrastructure reflects its rich cultural heritage and rapid development.

Lingaraj Temple

This 11th-century temple is one of the oldest and most prominent landmarks in Bhubaneswar, dedicated to Lord Shiva. Its intricate carvings and towering structure make it a masterpiece of Kalinga architecture.

Time to Explore: 45 mins

Closed on: Open on all days

Entry Fee: No Entry Fee

Dhauli Shanti Stupa

Located on the banks of the Daya River, this peaceful Buddhist monument marks the site of the Kalinga War and symbolizes Emperor Ashoka’s embrace of Buddhism.

Time to Explore: 90 mins

Closed on: Open on all days

Entry Fee: ₹25 per person

Nandankanan Zoological Park

Spread over 437 hectares, this zoo and botanical garden is famous for its white tigers and safari tours. It’s a favorite destination for nature and wildlife lovers.

Time to Explore: 180 mins

Closed on: Monday

Entry Fee: ₹100 per person

Odisha State Museum

This museum offers a deep insight into Odisha’s cultural and historical legacy through its rich collection of sculptures, coins, manuscripts, and traditional instruments.

Time to Explore: 60 mins

Closed on: Monday

Entry Fee: ₹20 per person
