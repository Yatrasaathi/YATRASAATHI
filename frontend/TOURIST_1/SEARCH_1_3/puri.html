Puri

A coastal city along the Bay of Bengal, Puri is famed for the Jagannath Temple, a significant Hindu pilgrimage site. It's also known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant annual festivals. The city seamlessly blends spiritual heritage with natural beauty, attracting visitors worldwide.

Jagannath Temple

One of India's most revered Vaishnava sites, this temple is dedicated to Lord Jagannath (a form of Krishna). The main shrine was constructed in the 10th century by Anantavarman of the Chodaganga dynasty.

Time to Explore: 150 mins

Closed on: Open on all days

Entry Fee: No Entry Fee

Konark Sun Temple

A 13th-century temple dedicated to the Sun God, Surya. Designed as a massive stone chariot with 24 intricately carved wheels and seven galloping horses, it's a UNESCO World Heritage Site exemplifying Odisha's architectural brilliance.

Time to Explore: 90 mins

Closed on: Open on all days

Entry Fee: ₹35 per person

Golden Beach

Located along the Bay of Bengal, this pristine beach is known for its golden sands and mesmerizing sunrises. It's a popular spot for both relaxation and cultural events.

Time to Explore: 60 mins

Closed on: Open on all days

Entry Fee: ₹30 per person

Wonder World Water Park

Eastern India's largest water park and resort, offering thrilling water rides, serene accommodations, and unique dining experiences, making it a perfect destination for family fun.

Time to Explore: 180 mins

Closed on: Open on all days

Entry Fee: ₹799 per person
